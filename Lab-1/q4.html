ROYAL ENFIELD CONTENENTAL GT 650

The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is widely considered one of the most accessible and stylish "Cafe Racers" in the world.
It’s part of Royal Enfield’s 650 Twin platform, designed to bring back the golden era of British motorcycling with modern reliability.
The GT 650 is perfect for weekend "canyon carving" and short, stylish city sprints. If you prefer long-distance touring with more comfort, its sibling, the Interceptor 650, offers the same engine but with a more upright, relaxed seating position.
